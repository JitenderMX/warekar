HOW WE WORK

we love to find solutions to complex challenges

1
FIXED DESIGN BRIEF
Given an inflexible design brief, we transform supplied plans into detailed architectural, structural, and utilities drawings, estimates, and tender documents within the client's budget. Our focus includes façade options and contractor/vendor selection.
2
FLEXIBLE DESIGN BRIEF
Ideal collaboration occurs when clients provide detailed briefs, remain flexible to layout, building, and utilities advice. We appreciate projects where mutual input results in superior design, exceeding the original brief.
3
OPEN DESIGN BRIEF
In projects with vague client ideas, we excel in crafting briefs and steering from concept to completion. We've navigated challenges, envisioning plots and floating tenders before land possession, ensuring client satisfaction.
